## Out of Season proposals

The _Out Of Season Proposals_ form is not addressing a specific event or deadline. It is always open, even when our official CFP for a DevOpsDays event is not.
{: .kicker}


[Propose Out Of Season stuff](/out-of-season-proposals/){: .btn .btn--primary target="_blank"}

It's a way for you to get _anything_ off your chest at _any_ point in time. So if you have an idea for something you'd like to present use that form.

We're always interested in gathering good ideas for presentations and we may even hand-pick stuff from this backlog to our official events. So it's also a way for you to be top-of-mind in the program committee's consideration.
