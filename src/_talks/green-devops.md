This talk explores the pivotal role of data and empirical research in driving sustainable DevOps practices, with a focus on CI/CD, cloud environments, and infrastructure management. Leveraging the Green Code Foundation Maturity Matrix, we'll showcase how organizations can assess and improve their sustainability efforts. The discussion highlights the shared responsibility between cloud providers and users to implement eco-friendly strategies, reducing energy consumption while maintaining performance. Attendees will gain insights into current trends, evidence-based practices, and real-world case studies that demonstrate the successful integration of sustainable strategies into DevOps workflows.
