We're happy to host or cross post your DevOps related posts here on our site. If you have written something share-worthy give it a spin here at our community page.
{: .kicker}

1. Create a fork of `devopsdays-dk/devopsdays.dk``
2. Write a post in the `_post` folder - use MarkDown (KramDown flavour)
3. Add Author bio+details in the `_data/authors.yml`
4. Commit and create a pull request
